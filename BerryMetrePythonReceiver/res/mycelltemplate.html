I MADE A SOLAR CELL!

My blueberry solar cell

I made a solar cell from blueberries and it just took me a few minutes. It generated a maximum of 0.1mW of energy. While this does not sound like much, it could replace 2 coin cell batteries every year. The most efficient dye-cells could replace around 100 batteries every year. That is 10x more than the average number of batteries every person in Britain uses.
